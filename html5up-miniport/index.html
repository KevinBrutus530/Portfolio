Hi, I'm Kevin Brutus.

I’m a Full Stack Web Developer based in NYC. In my past life working as a Housing Specialist, I assisted my clients by assessing their needs based on their current situation. Overtime, I became good at understanding their problems and connecting them to the best resources, impacting not only their housing, but livelihood as well. I was able to accomplish this thanks to my intuitive sense of organization and can-do attitude. To assist my clients I used a variety of web applications that would hang up at certain times or were unintuitive. Identifying this problem inspired me to want to do something to fix it. For the past year, I’ve been spending my nights and weekends learning software engineering and now I look forward to continuing to help people by creating intuitive and reliable applications, ideally in civic tech.
